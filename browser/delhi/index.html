Delhi
7 Days 6 Nights

Delhi, city and national capital territory, north-central India. The city of Delhi actually consists of two components: Old Delhi, in the north, the historic city; and New Delhi, in the south, since 1947 the capital of India, built in the first part of the 20th century as the capital of British India.
One of the country’s largest urban agglomerations, Delhi sits astride (but primarily on the west bank of) the Yamuna River, a tributary of the Ganges (Ganga) River, about 100 miles (160 km) south of the Himalayas. The national capital territory embraces Old and New Delhi and the surrounding metropolitan region, as well as adjacent rural areas. To the east the territory is bounded by the state of Uttar Pradesh, and to the north, west, and south it is bounded by the state of Haryana.

Day 01 : Arrival Delhi,First you will arrive into the Indian capital, Delhi. Here a representative will attend you to your luxury hotel. Early in the morning, we will start by visiting Raj Ghat, the memorial to Mahatma Gandhi, walk around old Delhi including the colorful bazaar of Chandani Chowk, and Jama Masjid, the largest mosque in India. Drive through New Delhi, the capital created by the British. And then we will visit Qutub Minar tower and the vestiges of Medieval University at Hauz Khas.

Day 02 : Delhi,On the second dayof this tour, we will visit Delhi Golf Club which was redesigned by Peter Thompson in 1977 - 78. This golf club in Delhi was selected to host the first ever golf tournament at the Asian Games in 1982. The Club also has a nine hole B Course used first and foremost by novices. The first hole on the chief course is a 522 work areas, PAR 5, which doglegs to the left around a bristly bush rough.

Day 03 : Delhi, On the third day, visit to New Delhi sightseeing attractions including Qutub Minar that dates back to the 13th century. then we proceed to Humayun's Tomb and the India Gate. We will also visit past the Parliament House, majestic Rashtrapati Bhawan (President's mansion), Secretariat Building, Laxminarayan Temple and the renowned Connaught Place.

Day 04 : Delhi - Agra, Early in the morning, we will travel to Agra which was Mogul capital during the 16th and 17th centuries. After arriving in Agra, we will go to major attraction places in Agra including Agra market and Agra Fort and check in to your hotel. Finally end the day with a sunset; we will visit to Taj Mahal(Closed on Friday), described as the most beautiful monument to love ever made.

Day 05 : Jaipur, After having breakfast, we will move to the pink city, Jaipur. First we will visit the Amber Fort, a wonderful fortification palace. We will travel up the hillside by elephant, through the gates in time-grateful fashion.

Day 06 : Jaipur, On the sixth of this tour, we will round for golf at Rambagh Palace Polo and Golf Club, a 18 hole Par 72 course. In the afternoon you have got so many alternatives to walk around the sights of Jaipur. We will visit the sprawling City Palace complex, the Hawa Mahal (Palace of the Winds) with its complicated exterior lacework and the Jantar Mantar Observatory.

Day 07 : Jaipur - Delhi, After having breakfast, we will drive back to Delhi for your flight back to home.
