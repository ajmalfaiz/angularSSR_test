Singapore
3 Nights 4 Days

Singapore is much more than the sum of its numerous attractions. It’s constantly evolving, reinventing, and reimagining itself, with people who are passionate about creating new possibilities. It’s where foodies, explorers, collectors, action seekers, culture shapers, and socialisers meet―and new experiences are created every day. Don't stop at finding out what you can do when you visit. Let our Passion Ambassadors show you what you can be when you're here. Because we’re more than just a destination. We’re where passion is made possible.

DAY 01: ARRIVE SINGAPORE – JURONG BIRD PARK, Welcome to Singapore! It is also called as the Lion city. On arrival you will be met and transferred to the hotel for your check in (Check in is at 15:00 Hrs). In the afternoon proceed for a tour of Jurong Bird Park, featuring over 5000 birds from 380 species. This is the largest open concept park in the Asia Pacific region. The highlight of the park is its unique penguin feeding session and the Bird show. After the tour you will be transferred back to the hotel. Rest of the day is at leisure. Enjoy dinner at Indian restaurant. Overnight in Singapore

DAY 02: SINGAPORE – CITY TOUR + SENTOSA ISLAND TOUR,After breakfast at the hotel, proceed for a half a day tour of Singapore city tour. Drive past the Parliament House, Supreme Court, City Hall, China Town and Little India. Stop at Raffles landing site, one of the most historical sites. After the tour you will be dropped at the hotel. In the afternoon proceed for a visit to Sentosa Island – Singapore's playground which has something for the entire family. Enjoy one way cable car, Tiger Sky tower & Merlion Tower. End your day at Sentosa with the Award Winning show “Wings of Time” set against the majestic open sea!! Be mesmerized by a multi-sensory presentation of water display, laser show, fire effects and spectacular music as you soar through space and time in a magical adventure. Enjoy dinner at Indian restaurant. Overnight in Singapore

DAY 03: SINGAPORE – SINGAPORE ZOO, After breakfast at the hotel, proceed for a tour of Singapore Zoo. This is a magnificent 28-hectares Zoo houses more than 2800 animals from all over the world. It is based on an open concept with very few cages and where many of the animals are allowed to roam freely. Rest of the day is at leisure. Enjoy dinner at Indian restaurant. Overnight in Singapore

DAY 04: SINGAPORE – – GARDENS BY THE BAY, After breakfast at your hotel, proceed for a tour of Gardens by the Bay tour. It is a garden spread around 101 hectares and houses more than 250 thousand rare plants. The garden has many attractions like cloud forest which is a mountain covered with diverse vegetation cloaking the world’s tallest indoor waterfall, flower dome, super tree grove and many more. Rest of the day is at leisure. Enjoy dinner at Indian restaurant. Overnight in Singapore

DAY 05: DEPART SINGAPORE, After breakfast at the hotel, the day is at leisure (Standard heck out time is at 12:00 Noon). Later you will be transferred to the airport for your flight back home.
