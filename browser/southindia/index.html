South India Tour
9 Days 8 Nights

Our 9 days South India tour package provides you the best chance to explore various attractions and places to visit in South India. South India is known for its beautiful natural surroundings, traditional lifestyle and religious heritage. This South India tour package covers major tourist destinations in Tamil Nadu and Kerala. Get an enchanting experience for your travel in South India and enjoy your South India holidays with Splendor Holidays.
The majority of the people in South India speak one of the four major Dravidian languages: Telugu, Tamil, Kannada and Malayalam. During its history, a number of dynastic kingdoms ruled over parts of South India whose invasions across southern and southeastern Asia impacted the history and culture in those regions. Major dynasties that were established in South India include the Cheras, Cholas, Pandyas, Pallavas, Satavahanas, Chalukyas, Rashtrakutas and Vijayanagara. Some Western European countries established ports in the region by military coercion in the 18th and 19th centuries.

Day 01 : Arrival at Coimbatore,On your arrival at Coimbatore airport our representative/chauffeur will welcome you at the arrival terminal/gate. Then proceed to Ooty which is approximately 100 kms from the airport.Check-in to your hotel in Ooty. The remaining day is free for enjoying the evening leisure and some shopping in the town. Night halt at the hotel.

Day 02 : Ooty Local Sightseeing,Morning after a healthy breakfast from the hotel go for full day sightseeing in Ooty. Visit the tourist destinations of Ooty which includes Botanical Garden, Rose Garden, Doddabetta peak and Emerald Lake. After your lunch visit 7th and 9th mile which are the famous film shoot locations in Ooty. Evening enjoy the boating in Ooty Lake. Come back to the hotel and overnight stay over there.

Day 03 : Ooty - Palani - Kodaikanal, Check-out the hotel after the breakfast and then proceed to Visit Palani Murugan Temple ,which is one of the Six Abodes of Lord Murugan. After the lunch proceed to kodaikanal, which is named as ‘the gift of forest’. On your arrival at kodaikanal check-in to your hotel booked for you. Enjoy the evening leisure and overnight stay over there

Day 04 : Kodaikanal Sightseeing, t your breakfast from the hotel and go for full day sightseeing in Kodaikanal. Visit Shenbaganur Museum, Coakers Walk, Dolphin Nose, Green Valley View, Pillar rock, Pine Forest and Kodai Lake. Evening back to hotel. Overnight stay hotel at Kodaikanal.

Day 05 : Kodaikanal - Madurai - Munnar, Morning after your breakfast check-out the hotel. Then proceed to Madurai, one of the oldest cities in India. Visit very famous Temples like Meenakshi Amman Temple, Mariamman Teppakulam temple and Thiruparankundram Murugan Temple in Madurai. These temples attracts both tourists and pilgrims alike. Also visit Gandhi Museum one of the five Gandhi Museums in India. After the visit proceed to Munnar,the Kashmir of south India. On your arrival at Munnar check-in to your hotel/resort in Munnar. Night halt at the hotel.

Day 06 : Munnar Sightseeing, Go for a full day sightseeing in Munnar after the breakfast from the hotel. Visit Mattupetty Dam which offers many fascinating sights to relish, the Shola forests around Mattupetty area, Echo Point and top station of Munnar. After the lunch visit Eravikulam National Park where you can see the rare Nilgiri Tahr, Elephant Arrival Spot, View Point & the green carpet of Tea Gardens. Later return to Munnar and see the evening market of old Munnar. Come back to the hotel and night halt in the hotel.

Day 07 : Munnar - Alleppey, After having your breakfast check-out the hotel and proceed for your backwater cruise in Alleppey. The backwaters are unique products of Kerala and are found nowhere else in the world. Alleppey is also known as Venice of the East by its special geographical features as the place is famous for its backwater stretches, narrow canals and paddy fields. Check-in to your traditional Houseboat in the Vembanad lake at around 12 pm. Enjoy the full day in the Houseboat starting from lunch, evening tea snacks and dinner and overnight stay in the Houseboat.

Day 08 : Alleppey - Athirapally - Guruvayoor, Enjoy the morning in the houseboat viewing the natural vistas and the sightseeing spots. After the breakfast check-out the houseboat at 9 AM. Later proceed to visit Athirapally Waterfalls, one of the beautiful waterfalls in India. You can reach top of the waterfall by 5 minutes walk from the main road. See the waterfall drops from a height of 80 feet through several parallel streams and experience the natural beauty of waterfall. Evening proceed to Guruvayoor. Check-in to your hotel in Guruvayoor and overnight stay over there.

Day 09 : Guruvayoor - Cochin, Early morning visit the Lord Krishna temple to offer prayer and seek blessings. After the breakfast check-out the hotel. You can also visit elephant camp in the Punnathur kotta which is just 3 kms from the Guruvayur temple. After the visit we drive you back to Cochin airport to catch your flight.
